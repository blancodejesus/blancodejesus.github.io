Our Story

Hailing from Puerto Rico and Venezuela, Andrea and Andrés like to believe destiny had some city-planning of its own. Their paths crossed in New York City as Andrea was beginning her master’s in public health and Andrés was starting his career as a software engineer.

What began as a chance meeting soon turned into a life filled with shared moments. From creating a home together with their miniature schnauzer, Arepa, to traveling near and far, they have built countless memories side by side.

At their happiest, you will find them cooking something delicious, binge watching their favorite series, or dancing through the living room to salsa and reggaetón. Wherever they are, love, laughter, and good music always follow.

July 18th, 2026, We Said Yes!

Andrea said forever yes to Andrés at the Laguna of Condado in San Juan, Puerto Rico, with the water shimmering around them and the city quietly bearing witness to the moment that changed everything.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

The dress code is garden party formal. Think elegant and festive while still comfortable for an outdoor celebration. Light suits, cocktail dresses, flowing gowns, and dressy separates are all welcome. We encourage colors and prints that feel joyful and celebratory.

Is this a child-free wedding?

While we absolutely love your little ones, we have chosen to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. This allows everyone, including parents, to relax and fully enjoy the day and evening with us. We hope you understand and are able to join us for a night of celebration.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity and the nature of our celebration, we are only able to accommodate guests who are listed on the invitation. Those who have been extended a plus one will be notified directly. We have put a great deal of care and thought into creating our guest list and truly appreciate your understanding as we celebrate this special day.

What weather should I expect?

April in Puerto Rico is warm, sunny, and beautiful. You can expect daytime temperatures in the mid to high 80s, with mild evenings and a light coastal breeze. While brief tropical showers are always possible, April is generally dry and pleasant. Our venue is outdoors but fully roofed, so you will be comfortably covered while still enjoying the open air. We recommend light, breathable fabrics and comfortable shoes suitable for warm weather.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes. Complimentary transportation will be provided to and from the hotel for the main wedding events. We will share exact pickup times and locations closer to the date. Guests are also welcome to use Uber or Lyft, and renting a car is a great option if you plan to explore the island during your stay. There is parking available at the hotel and venues for those who choose to drive.
